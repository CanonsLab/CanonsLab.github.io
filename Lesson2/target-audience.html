Description:

Included in the audience for the weather site is a specific demographic of people. Listed below are all the catergories that people in the audience would fit in.

  • Age Group: 18 and older
  • Income Class: All
  • Gender: Male or Female
  • Employment Status: Employed or Unemployed
  • Geographic Location: Multiple regions in America
  • Organizations or Industries: Businesses in need of local information
  • Marital Status: Married, Single or Divorced
  • Education: Understanding of basic internet skills

Five Scenarios:

  • Scenario 1: One user may come to the sight to view the weather summary for their region on the home page simply by scrolling down the page.
  • Scenario 2: One user may want to navigate to another regions page to view the news section for that region to see if their is any upcoming events they want to go to.
  • Scenario 3: One user may prefer to view information found on the website on a different platform like facebook and would then be able to navigate to thunder bros facebook page by clicking the facebook icon found at the bottom of every page.
  • Scenario 4: One user may need to contact the weather and news station directly to report a problem they find on the website, which they will be able to do throught the contact information footer.
  • Scenario 5: One user may want to find if their is any upcoming storms in which they can navigate to the storm center page to find the latest updates on storm weather forecasts.
