Overview

Purpose

The overall goal of my website is to promote the rental services for customers that like to do their homework before getting to the island and to provide information about the services, requirements, and rental process. And to promote freindly and easy rental services.

Audience

The Audience to which the website will be aimed is tourists and local citizens who wish to have fun in Cosumel. These people could be older or younger but they must be adults. Listed below are the prequisites for a person who wishes to use our services.

  • You must have at least $50 for a security deposit on one of our rental vehicles
  • You must have a drivers license
  • You must be over 12 to operate or ride the rental scooters
  • You must notify the rental company 24 hours before your requested rental to recieve the reservation rates
  • You must be able to perform transactions through google pay, cash, credit card, debit card or Paypal
  • You must be able to return the rental vehicle at either office location after your rental period is over
